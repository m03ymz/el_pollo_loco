Welcome to El Pollo Loco

You are a fearless adventurer from Mexico, facing the ultimate challenge:
battling the largest chickens to ever roam the desert!
Armed with nothing but your sharp salsa sauce, you can take on these oversized poultry beasts.
But even the smaller and medium-sized chickens can be defeated by bravely jumping on them. Find the leader of this feathered horde and let your salsa flow!
But be careful, for salsa is precious in the desert - don't waste it carelessly.
Let the adventure begin!
PEPE Pepe, a brave adventurer from Mexico, wields salsa sauce to battle giant desert chickens. He must defeat the leader without wasting his precious salsa.
NORMAL CHICKEN This enemy chicken roams the desert, ready to clash with Pepe. Tough and aggressive, it challenges any adventurer who dares to cross its path.
SMALL CHICKEN This small, pesky chick may be young, but it’s quick and relentless. Beware of its swift pecks and nimble moves as it defends its territory.
ENDBOSS CHICKEN The Giant Chicken is a colossal menace, towering over the desert. Powerful and fearsome, it leads the feathered horde and presents the ultimate challenge for Pepe as the Endboss.
COIN These shiny coins are scattered throughout the desert. Collect them to complete your collection and enhance your adventure.
SALSA BOTTLE Collect these salsa bottles scattered across the desert. Only the collected bottles can be thrown. You’ll see your collected amount on a bar—don't waste them! Use them to ultimately take down the Endboss.
